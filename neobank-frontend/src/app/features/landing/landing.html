Zero-trust architecture

Your money, behind military-grade walls

Security isn't a feature at NeoBank — it's the foundation. Every layer of our infrastructure is built to protect your assets and your data.

fingerprint
Biometric authentication

Every login and high-value transaction requires biometric confirmation — face ID, fingerprint, or hardware security key. No exceptions.

enhanced_encryption
AES-256 end-to-end encryption

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256. Even our own engineers cannot read your transaction data without your keys.

hub
Multi-Party Computation (MPC)

No single server holds your full private key. Keys are sharded across geo-distributed nodes — a compromise of one node reveals nothing.
